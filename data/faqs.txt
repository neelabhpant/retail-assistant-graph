Question: What is your return policy?
Answer: Our return policy allows you to return items within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. Items must be in their original packaging and condition. Some exclusions apply.
---
Question: How can I track my order?
Answer: You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the 'Order History' page. A tracking number will be provided once your order has shipped.
---
Question: Do you ship internationally?
Answer: Yes, we ship to most countries. International shipping rates and times vary depending on the destination. Please proceed to checkout to see the options available for your location.
---
Question: What payment methods do you accept?
Answer: We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay.
---
Question: How do I change the password for my account?
Answer: To change your password, please log in to your account, navigate to the 'Account Settings' page, and select the 'Change Password' option.
---
Question. What is the warranty on electronics?
Answer: All of our electronic products come with a one-year manufacturer's warranty that covers defects in materials and workmanship. This does not cover accidental damage.
